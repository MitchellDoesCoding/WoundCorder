Add the following keys to your app target's Info.plist with non-empty strings:

- NSCameraUsageDescription
  Value: "This app uses the camera to capture wound images and enable AR measurement."

- NSPhotoLibraryAddUsageDescription
  Value: "This app saves snapshots to your photo library."

- NSPhotoLibraryUsageDescription
  Value: "This app needs access to your photo library to save and access snapshots."

- NSMicrophoneUsageDescription
  Value: "Microphone access may be used when recording videos or for future features."

- NSLocationWhenInUseUsageDescription
  Value: "Location may be used to tag measurements with context (optional)."

Note: UIRequiresFullScreen is deprecated in iOS 26.0 and can be removed from the Info.plist if present.
